Arizona's First All Girls Market

Rodeo Rose Market is a western-inspired shopping event made for girls who love fashion, small businesses, cute finds, and community.

Become a Vendor

Shop Western, Local, and Girly

From boots and bows to handmade goods and local vendors, Rodeo Rose brings together a fun market experience with a stylish western vibe.

Our market is designed to highlight women-owned businesses, creative makers, and shoppers who want an event that feels welcoming, trendy, and memorable.
